Applied cryptography and other randomness...


Hello, and welcome to my blog! Here you might find me musing about a wide range of topics on my mind including cryptography, human factors (e.g., UI/UX and HCI), programming languages, math, music, other nerdy and senseless ramblings: really, anything more informal than a paper or other academic presentation that I find interesting. Throughout, I'll try my best to cite further sources for both academics and normal people to continue reading. :P

I don't claim to be an expert in most (or any) of the topics covered in these posts; my goal here is to motivate and demonstrate my own learning just as much as it is to share information which accessibly supports others'. Also, don't expect to see me talk much about my personal life here... I'll leave that to other communication channels. (is this a relief? disappointment? I'll let you decide that :)
