Applied cryptography, and other randomness...


Hello, and welcome to my blog! Here you might find me musing about a wide range of topics including cryptography, human factors (e.g., UI/UX), programming languages, math, music, other nerdy and senseless ramblings: really, anything that's on my mind that doesn't want to stay that way...

I don't claim to be an expert in most (or any...) of the topics covered in these posts; my goal here is to motivate and demonstrate my own learning just as much as it is to share information—usually in a more approachable manner than related research papers—which supports others'. Also, don't expect to see me talk much about my personal life here... I'll leave that to other communication channels. (is this a relief? disappointment? I'll let you decide that :)
