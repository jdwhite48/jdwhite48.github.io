Cryptography and other Randomness: A Blog


Welcome! Here you'll find me musing about a wide range of topics on my mind including cryptography, human factors (particularly, UI/UX and HCI), programming languages, math, and other nerdy and senseless things: really, anything more informal than a paper or other academic presentation that I find interesting. I'll be sure to cite (hopefully accessible) sources for both academics and normal people to continue reading. :P

I don't claim to be an expert in most of the topics covered in these posts; my goal here is to motivate and demonstrate my own learning as much as it is to share information which supports others'. I hope you'll join me in learning something new! :)
