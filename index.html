Academics

As a 2nd-year PhD Student and member of the BARC research lab at Purdue, I am conducting academic research under my advisor, Dr. Christina Garman.

My current research interests focus on the efficient design and implementation of privacy-enhancing cryptographic systems. My goal is to make it easier to design and deploy real-world cryptographic systems which simultaneously support both user privacy and accountability.
